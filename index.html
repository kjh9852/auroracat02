Cat Planet Story

It has been said that Aurora is a dreamland for Aurora cats, a mysterious realm that has been regarded by many as Mecca.
The story of this secret place begins when Aurora cats from different planets went on a journey to Aurora.
However, to reach this mystifying place safely, Aurora cats need your help by minting a total of 10,000 NFTs of these adorable felines!

Our plan

As a result of the global pandemic, many pet owners have unfortunately abandoned their pets.
We will raise public awareness of this situation and provide solutions to financially support related NGOs.
Our first step is to donate the profits from Aurora Cat NFTs to animal shelters and other animal protection organizations once we reach a target goal of $30,000.
